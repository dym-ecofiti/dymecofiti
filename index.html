Who We Are

Environmental Excellence at Scale

DYM Ecofiti is an emerging environmental and sustainability consultancy headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. Founded and led by a specialist practitioner, we bring deep technical expertise and a commitment to rigorous, locally-grounded solutions — serving government bodies, Communities, NGOs, development finance institutions and private clients across Kenya.

🌍
Our Mission

To advance sustainable development in Kenya through evidence-based environmental consultancy that protects ecosystems and delivers measurable results for clients and communities.

🔬
Our Expertise

A founder-led consultancy combining hands-on technical practice with strategic advisory — bringing specialist knowledge across ecology, hydrology, waste management and project delivery.

🤝
Our Clients

We work with Kenya's national and county governments, communities, UN agencies, international NGOs, development finance institutions, and forward-thinking private sector organisations.
