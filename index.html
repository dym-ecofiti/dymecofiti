Who We Are

Driving Sustainable Development at Scale

DYM Ecofiti Consulting Ltd is a Kenyan environmental and sustainability consultancy committed to helping organizations and communities navigate today's environmental and development challenges through practical, innovative and science-based solutions. Founded on the belief that sustainable development requires a balance between environmental stewardship, social responsibility and economic growth, we work alongside governments, development partners, NGOs and private sector organizations to design and implement projects that create lasting impact. From environmental compliance and climate resilience to ESG advisory, water resources management and project delivery, our focus is on providing solutions that are technically sound, locally relevant and aligned with global sustainability best practices.

🌍
Our Mission

To advance sustainable development by delivering innovative, evidence-based environmental, climate, ESG and project management solutions that protect natural resources, strengthen community resilience and create long-term value for our clients and society.

🔬
Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in environmental, climate and ESG advisory, recognized for delivering transformative solutions that inspire resilient communities, sustainable businesses and responsible development across Kenya and the wider African region.

🤝
Our Clients

We proudly partner with national and county governments, development partners, international NGOs, UN agencies, USG agencies, private sector organizations, financial institutions, community-based organizations and local communities to deliver sustainable, high-impact solutions that address environmental, climate and development challenges.
